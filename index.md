I'm an assistant professor of philosophy at [Indiana University Bloomington](https://philosophy.indiana.edu/index.html). I received my PhD from [Yale](https://philosophy.yale.edu) in 2023. In 2025, I was a Humboldt Fellow based at the [Center for Human Abilities](https://www.human-abilities.de) in Berlin. My work focuses on metaphysics, epistemology, and philosophy of mind in the early modern period. I'm originally from [Bozeman, Montana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bozeman,_Montana).
